About

"Big Grain" company

Our company is an important player on the agricultural export market. Over the past 9 years, we have been able to establish ourselves as a reliable partner not only in Russia, but also in the CIS countries, as well as near and far abroad.

History

Founded in 2014, the company has rapidly expanded its activities and today it is a leader in its field.
In 2022, it was rebranded in order to improve perception among the target audience and adapt to changes in challenging market conditions. Our company was named "Big Grain". This step led to changes in business strategy and, therefore, strengthening our position on the global market.

Our areas of focus

Wide range of agricultural export
  • - Oil and small-seeded crops
  • - Legume and grain crops
  • - Crops
  • - Grain crops

  • - Sunflower oil
  • - Sunflower meal
